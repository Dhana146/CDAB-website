About Us

Complete Dimension Association for the Blind (CDAB) is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting the blind and visually impaired community by providing practical, skill-based training, access to modern resources, and personalized support. We believe that every individual, regardless of their ability, has the right to live independently and contribute to society in meaningful ways.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: To empower the visually impaired by equipping them with the tools and resources necessary to lead independent, financially stable, and socially integrated lives. We work with communities, businesses, and policymakers to create a truly inclusive environment.

What We Do

CDAB focuses on practical, hands-on solutions that deliver real impact. Here’s how we do it:

  • Skill Development Programs: We offer tailored vocational training in areas like IT, craft-making, digital marketing, and more, helping participants build marketable skills that lead to sustainable jobs.
  • Entrepreneurship Support: For those who aspire to start their own ventures, we provide mentorship, seed funding, and business management training.
  • Assistive Technology Access: We ensure our community has access to cutting-edge assistive technologies, such as screen readers, braille devices, and smartphone apps that enhance independence.
  • Job Placement Services: Through partnerships with various industries, we help match our members with employers who recognize and value their unique capabilities.

Our Impact

Over the years, CDAB has successfully trained over 500 visually impaired individuals, of whom more than 70% have secured stable employment or started their own businesses. Our initiatives continue to create a ripple effect, transforming lives across the country.

We’ve also made significant strides in advocating for accessible workplaces, inclusive policies, and educational opportunities for the blind, ensuring that no one is left behind.

Get Involved

Want to make a difference? Whether you’re an individual looking to volunteer or a company interested in inclusive hiring, get in touch with us. Together, we can build a future where blindness is not a barrier but a strength.
