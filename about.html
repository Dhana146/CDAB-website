About us

The Complete Dimension Association for the Blind (CDAB) was established with the goal of empowering blind Nepali citizens both within and beyond the borders of Nepal. CDAB aims to equip visually impaired individuals to lead dignified lives by supporting them in achieving financial, social, cultural, and psychological well-being.

CDAB focuses on expanding opportunities in various fields of employment, self-employment, and entrepreneurship. The organization conducts in-depth research and provide support to open up a wide range of job sectors. CDAB's mission is to promote employment in diverse areas such as theater, information technology, finance, engineering, and medicine, transforming them into sustainable and advanced career options for the blind community.
