Welcome to CDAB

CDAB is dedicated to empowering blind and visually impaired individuals across Nepal. Our goal is to provide resources and opportunities that enable them to lead dignified, independent lives while fully participating in society.

Our Core Programs

We provide a wide range of services aimed at enhancing the skills and resources available to blind individuals. Our programs include:

  • Skill Development: Training programs in IT, handicrafts, and other fields to help the blind gain meaningful employment.
  • Entrepreneurship Support: Guidance and mentorship to assist visually impaired individuals in starting and growing their own businesses.
  • Assistive Technology: Access to the latest assistive devices such as braille readers and screen readers to improve quality of life.
  • Job Placement: Connecting visually impaired individuals with employers to foster inclusive work environments.

Our Impact

CDAB has positively impacted over 500 visually impaired individuals by helping them acquire necessary skills and secure employment. Through our efforts, 70% of our participants have found meaningful jobs or launched successful businesses.
