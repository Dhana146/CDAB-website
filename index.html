Welcome to CDAB

We are committed to creating opportunities for blind and visually impaired individuals in Nepal to live dignified, independent lives. Our mission is to remove barriers and provide resources to foster personal and professional growth in a fully inclusive society.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission and Vision

At CDAB, we strive to empower the visually impaired community by fostering financial, social, and psychological independence. Our vision is a society where visually impaired individuals can fully participate in all areas of life without limitation.

Our Core Programs

We offer a range of programs designed to equip visually impaired individuals with the skills and resources they need to thrive. Here are some of the ways we support our community:

  • Skill Development: Offering vocational training in IT, handicrafts, and digital marketing to open up employment opportunities.
  • Entrepreneurship Support: Providing guidance and mentorship for visually impaired entrepreneurs to start and sustain their businesses.
  • Assistive Technology: Ensuring access to the latest assistive devices, including screen readers and braille technology, to enhance daily living and career opportunities.
  • Job Placement: Connecting our members to inclusive employment opportunities in various sectors, including finance, technology, and healthcare.

Our Impact

Since our inception, CDAB has transformed the lives of over 500 visually impaired individuals, with more than 70% of them securing meaningful employment or starting their own businesses. Our work continues to create real change in communities across Nepal.
