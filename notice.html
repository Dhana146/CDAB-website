Final Results of the Membership Fee Renewal Poll

Dear Members,

We are excited to announce the final results of the recent poll conducted through our Messenger group regarding the renewal amount for the membership fee. Out of 33 eligible members, 19 members participated, representing a participation rate of 57.58%.

After reviewing the results, we are happy to confirm that the majority of votes were in favor of Rs 100, and as such, the membership renewal fee has been officially set at Rs 100.

Here is a summary of the voting results:

  • Rs 100 received 10 votes.
  • Rs 50 received 3 votes.
  • Rs 25 received 3 votes.
  • Rs 75 received 2 votes.
  • Rs 30 received 1 vote.

Best regards,

CDAB Team

Final Decision on Membership Fee

Dear members,

Thank you all for participating in the poll regarding the membership fee for our organization. The voting has now concluded, and I am pleased to announce the final result based on the majority's choice.

Here are the results:

  • Rs 100: 13 votes
  • Rs 500: 4 votes
  • Rs 300: 2 votes
  • Rs 365 (Per day Rs 1): 1 vote
  • Rs 1000: 1 vote

As the majority (13 members) voted for Rs 100, it has been decided that the membership fee for our organization will be Rs 100 per year.
